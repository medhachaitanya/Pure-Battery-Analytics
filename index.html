Battery Smart Charger - Pure Battery Add-On

Aug 13, 2023; 07:18PM PST

Are you tired of constantly monitoring your laptop's battery üîã and having to manually turn on or off your laptop's charger? Do you ever find yourself constantly checking your laptop's battery life to turn off the adapter after reaching your percentage? Then this app is for you!!

With the Battery Smart Charger - Pure Battery Addon, users can now enjoy the convenience of having their charger turn on or off automatically based on their preferred battery percentage, while still benefiting from the detailed battery analytics provided by Pure Battery Analytics.

Why did I build this app?
One of the top requests (survey) from the users of Pure Battery Analytics is to have a feature that automatically turns on or off the power supply (laptop adapter) based on the battery percentage of the laptop. Due to various limitations of the Windows UWP platform and OEM support, this was not possible to co This is where the latest add-on from Pure Battery comes in as the perfect solution to fulfill this request. No more overcharging or undercharging your laptop's battery, leading to longer battery life and less hassle.

More about the app: This app integrates a smart plug technology (using TP-Link switch) that automatically turns your charger on or off based on the battery percentage that you configure.
If you already have a smart plug from TP-Link, you're all set. Just download the app and login with your TP-Link account and set your desired percentage to turn on/off.
